Our Process

A methodology built for complexity and urgency.

01 · Week 1–2

Narrative Audit

We begin with deep listening—reviewing existing communications, interviewing key stakeholders, and mapping the gaps between your current narrative and the story you need to be telling.

02 · Week 3–4

Strategic Framing

We build the narrative architecture: your foundational story, message hierarchy, audience-specific variants, and the strategic framing that will guide all communication going forward.

03 · Week 5–8

Activation & Integration

We embed the new narrative across your critical touchpoints—leadership comms, website, fundraising materials, media strategy, and team alignment.

04 · Ongoing

Ongoing Advisory

We stay engaged as a strategic thinking partner—advising on communications as your work evolves, responding to emerging opportunities, and ensuring narrative consistency at scale.
