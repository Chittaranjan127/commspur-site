Terms & Notes

  1. Any changes or updates related to the website after delivery are chargeable, and will be quoted separately as per the subject and scope of the requested change.
  2. Payment terms: 25% (₹ 10,000) advance before development begins; the remaining 75% (₹ 30,000) payable after deployment.
  3. Delivery timeline: the website will be delivered within 1 week, counted from receipt of the advance payment and complete website content.
  4. Support window: 15 days of free bug-fix support after deployment. This covers defects in the delivered work only; new features, content changes, or enhancements remain chargeable as per note 1.
  5. Dependencies: the delivery timeline depends on timely inputs from the client — content, images, approvals, and any required credentials. Delays in client inputs extend the timeline accordingly.
  6. Cancellation: if the project is cancelled after development begins, the 25% advance is non-refundable; any work completed beyond the advance is billable proportionately.
  7. The above costing covers one design and development cycle of the agreed 5-page website; new pages, features, or third-party integrations are outside this scope.
  8. Hosting is covered for the first year; from the next year onward, hosting renewal is charged at ₹ 5,000 per year. Domain renewal is billed separately at actuals.
